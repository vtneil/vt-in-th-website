Vivatsathorn Thitasirivit (Neil)

Vivatsathorn Thitasirivit is currently enrolled at Chulalongkorn University, studying for his B.Eng. in computer engineering. His research is interdisciplinary and focused on computational theory, artificial intelligence, embedded system designs, nanosatellites, space systems and quantum computing. He was the co-founder of SPACE AC Institute of Technology, Assumption College and currently is a lecturer and an advisor.
